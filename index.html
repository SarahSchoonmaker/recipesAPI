Lasagna cake

Category: Italian

Subcategory: Diner

Rating: 5

See instructions

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef - 400 Grams
  • No-boil Lasagna Noodles - 1.0
  • Spaghetti Sauce - 800 Grams

Instructions

1) Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).\r\n\r\n2) Brown ground beef in a pan. Drain fat. Add tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Remove from heat.\r\n\r\n3) In a casserole dish, spread a layer of sauce mixture on the bottom. \r\n\r\n4) Spread a thin layer of ricotta cheese over the lasagne noodles and make a layer in the casserole dish, ricotta-side up. \r\n\r\n5) Spread a layer of sauce mixture over the noodles.\r\n\r\n6) Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.\r\n\r\n7) Repeat layers (ricotta-noodles, sauce, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used up. Make sure the top layer is mozzarella.\r\n\r\n8) Cover with aluminum foil and bake 35-45 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 15-20 minutes, until lightly browned.\r\n\r\n9) Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
